Why We Translate Mir Tankov

Welcome! You might be wondering how does this whole thing work? Well you are in the right spot to get your answer!

The mod is being made by a community of players that play Mir Tankov (Russian/Lesta version of World of Tanks). We are taking the in-game files, translating them by hand and then putting them inside of a .mtmod file (old .wotmod) - that's basically it!
